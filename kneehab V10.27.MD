# Knee Rehab v10.27 — Progression Consolidation

## Summary
Progression consolidation: hip hinge pattern added, graduated exercises promoted/demoted based on functional loading progress.

## Changes

### Added
- **Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift** — 2 × 8 each side, BW or light DB. Hip hinge pattern gap filled. Glute max, hamstrings (lengthened), erector spinae under single-leg standing load. Supports step-down via shared single-leg hip control. Regression: Kickstand RDL.

### Promoted to Main Block
- **Single-Leg Wall Sit** — replaces bilateral Wall Sit in Knee block. Bilateral graduated after consistent pain-free loading. Same Bellemans cartilage stimulus, higher unilateral demand.
- **Copenhagen Plank (Short Lever)** — replaces Adductor Side-Lying Isometric Hold in Glute block (both Load and Recovery days). Adductor ISO graduated after sustained pain-free tolerance.

### Moved to Optional
- **Short Arc Quad** — VMO now activating under functional load via step-down progression. Still available as targeted VMO primer.

### Dropped from Optional
- **Single-Leg Wall Sit** — promoted to main block
- **Copenhagen Plank** — promoted to main block

## Load Day Knee Block (v10.27)
1. Single-Leg Wall Sit — 5 × 20–30s, 45s rest
2. Slant Board Step Down — 2×10 primary, 2×8 secondary
3. Split Squat Progression — 2 × 8–10 each side, BW Ph1
4. Curtsy Squat — 3 × 8 weak / 2 × 8 strong
5. Cossack Squat — 3 × 5 weak / 2 × 5 strong
6. Hamstring Slide — 2 × 10, 4–6s eccentric
7. Standing Hamstring ISO — 3 × 20–30s each
8. Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift — 2 × 8 each side

## Load Day Glute Block (v10.27)
1. Copenhagen Plank — Short Lever — 3 × 20–30s each side
2. Hip Abduction — 3 × 12 weak / 2 × 12 strong
3. Single-Leg Glute Bridge — 2 × 10 each

## Optional Pool (v10.27, Pick 2)
- Hamstring Isometric in Flexion (posterior priority)
- Terminal Knee Extension
- Short Arc Quad (new to optional)
- Hanging Knee Raise
- Single-Leg Calf Raise — Straight Knee
- Single-Leg Calf Raise — Bent Knee (Soleus)

## Rationale
- No pain + progressing step-down = bilateral wall sit below threshold, SAQ primer role diminished
- Hip hinge pattern was missing — SLDL ties posterior chain under functional standing load
- Adductor ISO bridge served its purpose — Copenhagen is the natural progression
- Net exercise count in main block unchanged (dropped 2, promoted 1, added 1)
- Split squat volume unchanged at user request (2 × 8–10)
