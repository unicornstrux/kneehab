# Knee Rehab v11.0 — Rehab to Training Transition

## Summary
Major restructure: rehab program → training program. 4 day types (Load, Recovery, Activity, Daily Habits) → 3 day types (Day A Lower Body, Day B Upper Body, Activity). Upper body mandatory. Daily habits integrated. Zone 2 unrestricted.

## Day A — Lower Body (~45 min)
- T=0: Collagen + Vitamin C
- T+5: Primer (Tibialis Raise, Dead Hang)
- T+8: Mobility (90/90, Cat-Cow flow, Lizard Pose, Wall Ankle, Couch Stretch, Pigeon, Shoulder CARs)
- T+15: Spinal (Bird Dog — pick variation: Slow Return / Elbow-to-Knee / Bird Dog Row)
- T+17: Core (Hollow Body, Side Plank, Hip Flexor — pick one)
- T+21: Glute (Copenhagen Plank, Hip Abduction, SL Glute Bridge)
- T+24: Knee Block (SL Wall Sit, Step Down, Split Squat 2s pause, Curtsy OR Lateral Step-Up, Hamstring Slide, Standing Ham ISO, SL RDL, Soleus Calf Raise)
- T+41: Bellemans Bike (10-12 min)
- PM: Short Ruck optional (20-30 min, not same day as Zone 2)

## Day B — Upper Body (~40 min)
- T=0: Collagen + Vitamin C (required — upper body qualifies)
- T+5: Primer (Tibialis Raise, Dead Hang)
- T+8: Mobility (same as Day A — all daily)
- T+15: Spinal & Shoulder (Bird Dog variation, WLYT)
- T+18: Core (Hollow Body OR RKC Plank alternate, Side Plank, Hip Flexor)
- T+22: Knee Maintenance (SL Wall Sit 3×20s, Standing Ham ISO 2×20s, Copenhagen 2×20s)
- T+27: Upper Body (Scap Pull-Up, Eccentric Pull-Up, Ring Row, Push-Up Tempo, KB Press, Face Pull, Scap Push-Up)
- PM: Short Ruck optional

## Daily Habits (informal)
- Short Foot Isometric
- Single-Leg Balance
- Backward Walk
- Wall Slide

## Key Changes from v10.27
- Rehab → Training restructure (Day A/B split replacing Load/Recovery)
- Upper body mandatory on Day B with collagen
- Daily habits (Tibialis, Dead Hang) folded into Primer block
- Lizard Pose replaces World's Greatest Stretch
- Pigeon Pose replaces Supine Hip ER (regression noted)
- Bird Dog now has 3 variations (Slow Return, Elbow-to-Knee, Row)
- Hip Flexor exercise mandatory in Core (Hanging Knee Raise / Tuck L-Sit / Foot-Supported L-Sit)
- Cossack Squat replaced by Lateral Step-Up (alternates with Curtsy)
- Split Squat and Curtsy now include 2s pause at bottom (standard, not modifier)
- Soleus Calf Raise promoted to main Knee block
- WLYT moved to Day B only
- Hollow Body / RKC Plank alternate on Day B
- Zone 2 cycling unrestricted (collagen before any Zone 2)
- Short Ruck added (20-30 min evening, loaded pack)
- Knee maintenance isometrics on Day B (reduced volume)
